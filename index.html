A Game of Strategy and Skill

Cricket: The Gentleman's Game

Unlike many other sports, cricket emphasizes both physical prowess and mental sharpness. Players must excel in batting, bowling, or fielding, each requiring precision and technique. The game's unique formats—Test matches, One-Day Internationals (ODIs), and T20s—offer varied challenges and excitement.
